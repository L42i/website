# 25-26 GA3D Contest

In the 25-26 academic year, **UGA Dancz Center for New Music** and **Georgia Tech's School of Music** held the first **GA3D** composition contest.
Students from both universities were invited to submit electronic pieces of any style or genre that utilize Ambisonics 3D audio formats. 
Selected finalists' pieces were featured at a concert at the UGA Dancz Center Black Box Theatre in April 18, 2026.
